# XUAN Spatial Perception Product Marketing Context

## Product

XUAN Spatial Perception / 轩空间感知 is an exhibition-facing website and lightweight portfolio for a new media spatial design practice.

## Event Goal

Support the Guangzhou exhibition. Visitors should scan the site and understand the practice within 10 seconds.

## Audience

- Exhibition visitors
- Brand and commercial environment clients
- Cultural tourism and museum partners
- Potential collaborators
- Internal partners reviewing the site direction

## Core Message

空间为画布，光影即颜料。  
Space as Canvas, Light and Shadow as Material.

## Content Pillars

- XUAN / 轩空间感知: overall brand and spatial perception direction
- Tzone / 图域: cultural tourism, museum, exhibition, and brand environment projects
- Virtura: creative network, spatial media, live visuals, real-time systems
- Three directions: city to landscape, heritage to future, digital to physical

## Voice

Clean, direct, exhibition-like, restrained, precise.

Avoid:

- Long conceptual essays on the homepage
- "不是……而是……" contrast formulas
- Startup landing-page hype
- Heavy technical jargon
- Dense Chinese paragraphs

Prefer:

- Short labels
- Clear bilingual pairs
- Project evidence
- Scannable lists
- Generous spacing
